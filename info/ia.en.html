How it works: the health check (every 6h) measures fallbacks, timeouts and spend — if anything degrades, an alert reaches Telegram on its own and the override reverts to the baseline. The scout (daily 6:15) benchmarks the pool's real models against per-pattern standards; the tune applies the best one only after 2 consecutive passing scouts + a fresh canary + end-to-end verification (with rollback).
